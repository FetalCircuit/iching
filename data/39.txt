39: Obstructing

Seek counsel from someone you trust. He will assist you through your difficulties.	

Line One of This Hexagram is a 6: 

Be cautious and do not take action before the time is right.

Line Two of This Hexagram is a 6: 

Remain true to yourself and principles even in the face of adversity.

Line Three of This Hexagram is a 9: 

Bad times will pass.

Line Four of This Hexagram is a 6: 

Do not play the martyr. Ask for help when you feel overwhelmed.

Line Five of This Hexagram is a 9: 

Do not be afraid to ask for help.

Line Six of This Hexagram is a 6: 

Only assistance from those you trust will see you through. Do not act before you are prepared.
