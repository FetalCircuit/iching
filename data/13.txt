13: Assembling People

Quietly bring your allies together. Prepare to bring your ideas into reality. A great adventure lies before you.	

Line One of This Hexagram is a 9: 

Make new friends and partners. They will introduce you to prospects.

Line Two of This Hexagram is a 6: 

Do not be afraid of new partnerships and allies. 

Line Three of This Hexagram is a 9: 

Understand your competition. Do not strike until you can win.

Line Four of This Hexagram is a 9: 

Humility and goodwill are admirable qualities. Do not humiliate those whom you defeat.

Line Five of This Hexagram is a 9: 

Once your deeds are accomplished, celebrate your success with joy.

Line Six of This Hexagram is a 9: 

When you win, show compassion toward the loser.
