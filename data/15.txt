YOUR READING: QIAN

15: Modesty

Remain modest and do not be ruled by your pride. Act in a dignified manner regardless of your position.	

Line One of This Hexagram is a 6: 

Do not boast. Quietly move toward your goal.

Line Two of This Hexagram is a 6: 

Humility will insure good fortune.

Line Three of This Hexagram is a 9: 

Work quietly and diligently. 

Line Four of This Hexagram is a 6: 

Follow the examples of those you admire.

Line Five of This Hexagram is a 6: 

To insure wide-spread support, be fair to all.

Line Six of This Hexagram is a 6: 

Humility will guarantee great achievements. Speak to all who want to speak to you.
