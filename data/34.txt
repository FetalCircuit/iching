YOUR READING: DA ZHUANG

34: Great Strength

Your integrity, perseverance and thoroughness will bring you admiration. Set forth on new ideas.	

Line One of This Hexagram is a 9: 

Use your talents. Do not hold back or be modest. 

Line Two of This Hexagram is a 9: 

Take a stand and you will win the day.

Line Three of This Hexagram is a 9: 

Do not pursue unworthy endeavors. Focus on opportunities that will lift you to new heights.

Line Four of This Hexagram is a 9: 

Move when you are sure you are ready.

Line Five of This Hexagram is a 6: 

There will be setbacks, but you will persevere.

Line Six of This Hexagram is a 6: 

Be patient and your good work will eventually be rewarded.
