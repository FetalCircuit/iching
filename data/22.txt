22: Adorning

Seek new knowledge and awareness. Repair relationships and property.	

Line One of This Hexagram is a 9: 

Treat your body and spirit well. 

Line Two of This Hexagram is a 6: 

Give your best to all endeavors. Mind your appearance and bolster your self-esteem.

Line Three of This Hexagram is a 9: 

Spend your money to live better, but never forget material means are not an end unto themselves.

Line Four of This Hexagram is a 6: 

Do not take advantage of others. Insure your proposals benefit all parties concerned. Do not be flamboyant.

Line Five of This Hexagram is a 6: 

Do not develop a lavish lifestyle. Reap the rewards of investing your money wisely.

Line Six of This Hexagram is a 9: 

Do not overindulge. Keep things simple. Speak the truth.
